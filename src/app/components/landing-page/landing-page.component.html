Lender Compliance Program – Personal

LCP CORPORATE EDITION - ENROLLMENT FEE
(See Payment Plan Matrix below)
By enrolling in the Lender Compliance Program - Corporate, we will perform some or all of the following:
  • Work to remove any previous addresses reporting on the file
  • Ensure your current address is reporting correctly across all 3 credit bureaus
  • Add primary tradelines to all 3 credit bureaus to meet the criterion of having a minimum of 4 open primary tradelines
  • Add a revolving Primary Tradeline with a minimum $5,000 credit limit to meet this criterion
  • Remove any inquiries that did not result in credit being issued from all 3 credit reports
  • Target and remove any derogatory items reporting on all 3 credit bureaus
  • Target and attempt to update or reverse any late pays reporting on all 3 credit bureaus
  • Perform Data Suppression techniques to remove bankruptcies and or any other public records
  • Increase credit scores so that they meet lender requirements
  • Ensure that you meet all DTI requirements and advise how much income is needed in order to qualify for funding based on all outstanding debt
  • Ensure that your Average Age of Accounts (AAoA) meets lender requirements and calculating the amount of age that's needed, and provide a solution to meet this criterion.


Lender Compliance Program – Corporate

LCP CORPORATE EDITION - ENROLLMENT FEE
(See Payment Plan Matrix below)
By enrolling in the Lender Compliance Program - Corporate, we will perform some or all of the following:
  • *EVERYTHING in the Lender Compliance Program - PERSONAL
  • “Done-For-You” Corporate Credit Building
  • Ensure that your business name and industry meets lender compliance
  • Set up a lender compliant business address
  • Set up Business Phone
  • Register Business in the National 411 Database
  • Set up your EIN (if necessary)
  • Set up your Corporate Bank Account
  • Set up your Merchant Account
  • Set up your D&B, Equifax, and Experian Business Profiles
  • Design Company Logo
  • Set up Website Domain
  • Set up Business Email to meet lender compliance
  • Design and Develop Corporate Website
  • Set up and Provide 12 mos of Website Hosting
  • Set up and Design Social Media Websites
  • Target and remove any derogatory items reporting on all 3 business credit bureaus
  • Target and attempt to update or reverse any late pays reporting on all 3 business credit bureaus
  • Done-For-You Concierge Services
  • Monitor the status of your file 24/7/365 - 360 degree visibility
  • Corporate Funding Concierge - We serve as your very own corporate funding concierge providing you direct access to the Strategic Funding Program.
  • Build Corporate Credit to where your company will qualify for NO PG Funding
